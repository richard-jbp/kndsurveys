Our Story

KND Surveys is a family run business that has been providing a friendly and professional service for over 35 years.

  • After completing his formal education in 1972 Nigel Davies, the founder, began working for Amey Roadstone Limited as a setting out engineer on road improvement projects and the M40 in Buckinghamshire.
  • In 1973 he was offered a job as setting out engineer for Lovells undertaking work for major housing developments in the Home Counties and soon after was offered the job of supervising other setting out engineers throughout southern England.

Topographic/site surveys

KND Surveys Ltd are proud to be able to supply accurate, cost effective and fast surveys for small and large areas of land depending on our client’s requirements. We produce accurate surveys for many clients such as, landscape designers, architects, planners, developers or home owners.


Read More

Measured building surveys

KND Surveys have built an excellent reputation for providing detailed measure building surveys for over 35 years. We are able to provide floor plans, elevations and sections at any scale and to varying levels of detail, as required. If our client has a specification in keeping with their own working practices, we are able to work to this.


Read More

3D Scanning

With advancements in hardware and software, 3D Laser Surveys have become a quicker and more effective way of producing high accuracy survey drawings. This type of survey, using a portable scanner, remotely captures data on site. With up to a million points captured per second, this type of survey is particularly useful where there are areas hard to access or areas with ornate details.


Read More

Floor Areas

We have undertaken area calculations for clients ranging from a single property through to our clients with multi-million pound portfolios, providing NIA (Net Internal Area), GIA (Gross Internal Area) or GEA (Gross External Area) calculations. Because of our modern equipment and measuring methods we are able to provide fast collection of data on site, which is important when buildings are occupied and disruptions need to be kept to a minimum.


Read More

Subsidence monitoring and verticality surveys

KND Surveys Ltd has invested in high level technology equipment enabling us to produce an accurate monitoring of buildings with regard to verticality and precise level data collection. The use of accurate remote measuring equipment enables us to collect data on building facades where access is difficult or where details on adjacent buildings outside of the client’s ownership is required.
